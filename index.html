About Us

Who Am I?

Upon graduating with my BS in Mechanical Engineering, I joined one of the world’s most renowned construction companies. My responsibilities included analyzing stakeholder requirements, designing and procuring multiple systems while cultivating a positive working environment with my team members.

I started as a Junior Mechanical Engineer. Within two years, I was promoted to a Design Engineer, and shortly afterwards was given the opportunity to lead a mega project with Saudi ARAMCO.

With the rise of data-driven industries, I felt a need to be part of the new digital economy, so I started to familiarize myself with tools used by analysts today such as Python and SQL. To explore analytics further, I tapped into the cryptocurrency boom in 2017 and collaborated extensively with friends to leverage available data on numerous markets. While people were throwing away their life-savings in fear of missing out, we were exploring market patterns to seize arbitrage opportunities resulting from extreme market fluctuation.

I’m currently pursuing my MS in Business Analytics at the UC Davis GSM. Through our rigorous course, i’ve taken part in several projects that involve data visualization, machine learning and producing compelling presentations through data story-telling. I’m also working with a small tech start up as part of my practicum to help brands improve their product listings through detection of fraudulent reviews and drawing insights from review features.

My upbringing in a region vastly outnumbered by expats along with my numerous travels helped me a lot in understanding different thought processes and I strongly believe my refined gulf perspective and personality will have a lot to add to any team!
